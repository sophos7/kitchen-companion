# Chicken Stir Fry

Servings: 4

## Ingredients

- 500g chicken breast
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 2 bell peppers, sliced
- 200g broccoli florets
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, grated
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 200g rice
- salt to taste
- pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Cook rice according to package directions
1. Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces
1. Heat vegetable oil in a wok over high heat
1. Add chicken and cook until golden, about 5 minutes
1. Add garlic and ginger, stir for 30 seconds
1. Add bell peppers and broccoli, stir fry for 3-4 minutes
1. Add soy sauce and sesame oil, toss to coat
1. Season with salt and pepper
1. Serve over rice
