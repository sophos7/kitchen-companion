# Pasta Carbonara

Servings: 4

## Ingredients

- 400g spaghetti
- 200g bacon, diced
- 4 eggs
- 100g parmesan cheese, grated
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- salt to taste
- pepper to taste
- fresh parsley for garnish

## Instructions

1. Cook pasta according to package directions, reserve 1 cup pasta water
1. While pasta cooks, fry bacon until crispy
1. In a bowl, whisk eggs with parmesan and plenty of black pepper
1. Drain pasta and add to bacon pan (off heat)
1. Quickly pour egg mixture over hot pasta and toss vigorously
1. Add pasta water as needed for creamy consistency
1. Serve immediately with extra parmesan
