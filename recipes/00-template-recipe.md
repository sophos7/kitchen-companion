# Recipe Name

*with side dish and accompaniment*

Servings: 2

## Ingredients

- 200g salmon
- 500g potatoes
- 1 clove garlic
- 1 lemon
- 1 can chopped tomatoes
- 200g passata
- 1 sachet spice mix
- butter

## Instructions

### Get Prepared

1. Preheat the oven to 220°C/200°C fan/gas mark 7
1. Chop the potatoes into 2cm chunks
1. Peel and mince the garlic

### Cook the Main

1. Heat a drizzle of oil in a pan over medium-high heat
1. Season the salmon with salt and pepper
1. Cook for 4-5 mins on each side
1. Cook for 15 minutes or until golden [timer:15m]
1. Let rest for 5 minutes [5m]

### Finish and Serve

1. Plate up the salmon with potatoes alongside
1. Squeeze over the lemon juice
1. Season to taste
