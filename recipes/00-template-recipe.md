# Recipe Name

Servings: 4

## Ingredients

- 500g main protein
- 2 tbsp oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- salt to taste
- pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Prepare all ingredients
1. Cook for 15 minutes or until golden [timer:15m]
1. Let rest for 5 minutes [5m]
1. Season to taste
1. Serve and enjoy
